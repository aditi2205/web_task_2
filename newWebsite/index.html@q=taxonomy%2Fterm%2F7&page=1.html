Dr. Ranu Gadi

Biographical Sketch:  Dr. Ranu Gadi is presently heading the Applied Science Department and is working as Associate Professor at IGDTUW. She did her Masters and PhD in Chemistry from IIT Roorkee. She went to Environmental Sciences Division, Lancaster University, Lancaster, UK for Post Doctoral Fellowship. Prior to joining IGDTUW she was a Research Associate (CSIR) at the KDM Institute of Petroleum Exploration, Oil and Natural Gas Corporation, Dehradun and at the National Physical Laboratory, New Delhi. She is an active researcher and has set up Environmental Analysis and Research Lab at IGDTUW.She has twenty two years of research experience and more than twelve years of teaching experience. Two students have been awarded with Ph.D. degree under her supervision and presently four students are pursuing their Ph.D.  Achievements:  Conferred with the Third Best Researcher Award at GGSIP University, Delhi in September 2012 for the Academic year 2011-12. Awarded with the ‘Thomas Kuhn Honour Pin’ as hope for sustainable region for her research paper presented at the Third Int. Symposium on Non-CO2Green house Gases, 21st-23rdJanuary, 2002 at Maastricht, The Netherlands. Co-authored two books entitled, “Environmental Studies” and “Text Book of Environmental Studies”, Kataria Publishers, Delhi. Principal Investigator for a CSIR funded research project, Funding received: 18 lakhs. Principal Investigator for an ongoing DST funded research project, Funding received: 52 lakhs, Tenure: three years from Jan. 2015.
Associate Professor & Chief Warden
  • Applied Sciences And Humanities
Qualifications: Ph.D. (Chemistry) IIT Roorkee, Post Doctoral Fellowship (Environmental Sciences) Lancaster University, Lancaster, U.K.

Specialization: 

Research Interests: Atmospheric Chemistry and Climate Change, Biomass emissions, Characterization of atmospheric aerosols (elemental, organics, carbonaceous, metals), Environmental Analytical Chemistry, Speciation and Bioremediation of priority pollutants in water/wastewater.

Dr. Chhaya Ravikant

Biographical Sketch:  Dr. Chhaya Ravi Kant is working as Associate Professor (Physics) at IGDTUW. Dr. Bhavani Prasad

Biographical Sketch:  Dr. Bhavani Prasad is presently working as assistant Professor at IGDTUW. Prior to this he worked as post-doctoral research fellow at the Zhejiang University, China under the supervision of Prof. Hong Zhanglian. He received his PhD in 2013, from Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee, under the guidance of Dr. Raj Kumar Dutta, where he carried out studies on antibacterial and photocatalytic applications of ZnO nanoparticles. He is currently working on the photocatalytic properties and applications of graphene oxide-metal oxide nanocomposites. His scientific interests focus on synthesis and tuning of graphene-metal oxides nanocomposites and to study the effect of their optoelectronic and morphological properties in photocatalysis, water splitting and super capacitors etc.  Achievements/Awards:  Holder of Zhejiang University Postdoctoral fellowship, China. Holder of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) fellowship during Ph.D. Holder of Rajiv Gandhi National Fellowship (RGNF) while pursuing M. Tech.
