Prof. Ela Kumar

Prof. Ela Kumar, is presently head of Dept of CSE.  She obtained her B.E. in Electronics and Communication from Roorkee University, M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Roorkee University and Ph.D from Delhi University. She has done Ph.D in the area of Artificial Intelligence. She has a professional experience of 26 years . She has published more than   68  research papers in international and national journals and conferences. She has authored books and she is also a members of editorial  board of Journals.
